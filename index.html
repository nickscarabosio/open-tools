Client-facing prompts

Useful prompts I can hand a client without a whole explanation.

5 prompts

Click any prompt to expand the exact text.

1 Email recap client update 
Write a crisp client update email that covers:
- what happened
- what it means
- what we need from the client
- the next step
Keep it confident, short, and easy to forward.
2 Decision memo strategy 
Turn the notes below into a simple decision memo with:
- context
- options
- tradeoffs
- recommendation
- owner and timing
3 Meeting recap follow-up 
Summarize this meeting for a client in this format:
- decisions made
- open questions
- risks
- owners
- next actions
Use plain language and no fluff.
4 Prep brief before a call 
Create a short prep brief for an upcoming client call:
- who’s in the room
- what matters most
- likely tension points
- suggested questions
- the outcome we want
5 Polished rewrite edit mode 
Rewrite the draft below so it feels:
- sharper
- warmer
- more direct
- still human
Keep the original meaning, just make it cleaner.
