## featured toolwhy Hermes belongs here
HERMES agent layer

Hermes is the agent I use when the work needs follow-through.

It is useful when you want more than an answer. Hermes can remember context, use tools, keep going across steps, and come back with a result instead of stopping at a suggestion. That makes it a better fit for research, drafting, triage, and operational work that needs action.

## how we work togethersimple operating model
01 context

You give the goal.

Short is fine. Hermes figures out the shape of the task, pulls in relevant context, and starts moving.

02 work

Hermes does the legwork.

It can look things up, draft, organize, compare, or break a problem down without you managing every step.

03 result

You get the output or the next decision.

The point is to reduce friction: less back-and-forth, fewer dead ends, more momentum toward a finished result.
